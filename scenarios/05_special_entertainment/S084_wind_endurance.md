## Problem Definition

**Setup**: A validation drone must fly a 100 m straight-line trajectory along the $x$-axis at
altitude $z = 10$ m under a gusty wind field modelled by the Dryden turbulence spectrum. The mean
wind blows laterally (in the $y$-direction) at $\bar{W} = 12$ m/s; random gusts are superimposed
with standard deviation $\sigma_w = 4$ m/s. During the mission the drone runs an onboard Recursive
Least Squares (RLS) estimator that converges to the mean wind component and cancels it via a
feedforward force command. An LQR controller handles the residual dynamics. Roll and pitch are
hard-clamped to $\theta_{lim} = 30°$ to protect the airframe; the mission fails if any attitude
angle exceeds $40°$ or if lateral position error exceeds 5 m.

**Roles**:
- **Drone**: single quadrotor following a 100 m straight-line reference along $x$ at fixed altitude;
  subject to Dryden gust forces; controlled by LQR with optional RLS feedforward.
- **Wind field**: Dryden turbulence model generating coloured lateral and vertical gust velocities
  $W_y(t)$, $W_z(t)$ with spatial scale length $L_w = 200$ m and airspeed $V = 12$ m/s; mean lateral
  wind $\bar{W}_y = 12$ m/s added to the turbulence realisation.
- **RLS estimator**: online parameter estimator that treats the residual acceleration error as a
  slowly varying wind bias and adapts with a forgetting gain $\Gamma$.

**Objective**: Complete the 100 m straight-line flight while satisfying:
1. Lateral deviation $|y(t)| \leq 5$ m at all times; mission fails on first violation.
2. Attitude angles $|\phi(t)|, |\theta(t)| \leq 40°$ at all times; mission fails on first violation.
3. Minimise RMS lateral deviation and peak attitude angle over the full flight.

**Comparison strategies**:
1. **PID only** — standard proportional-integral-derivative position controller; no wind knowledge;
   expected to accumulate large lateral drift under sustained 12 m/s mean wind.
2. **LQR (no feedforward)** — optimal state-feedback controller; no wind estimation; reduced drift
   versus PID but still limited by the uncompensated mean wind component.
3. **LQR + RLS feedforward** (proposed) — LQR plus an online RLS estimator that converges to the
   mean wind acceleration and cancels it via $u_{ff} = -m\hat{w}$; expected to achieve the tightest
   lateral tracking with lowest attitude excursions.

## Mathematical Model

### Dryden Turbulence Model

The lateral gust velocity $W_y(t)$ is generated by passing white noise $n(t)$ through a
Dryden shaping filter. The continuous transfer function for the lateral component is:

$$W_y(s) = \sigma_w \sqrt{\frac{2V}{\pi L_w}} \cdot
  \frac{1 + \sqrt{3}\,\dfrac{L_w}{V}\,s}{\left(1 + \dfrac{L_w}{V}\,s\right)^2} \cdot N(s)$$

where $V$ is the nominal airspeed (m/s), $L_w$ is the turbulence scale length (m), $\sigma_w$ is
the gust standard deviation (m/s), and $N(s)$ is unit-intensity white noise. The discrete
approximation uses a second-order state-space realisation with matrices:

$$A_g = \begin{pmatrix} -2/\tau_g & -1/\tau_g^2 \\ 1 & 0 \end{pmatrix}, \quad
  B_g = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 0 \end{pmatrix}, \quad \tau_g = L_w / V$$

$$C_g = \sigma_w \sqrt{2V/(\pi L_w)} \begin{pmatrix} \sqrt{3}/\tau_g & 1/\tau_g^2 \end{pmatrix}$$

The total lateral wind is $W_y^{total}(t) = \bar{W}_y + W_y^{turb}(t)$, and vertical gust $W_z$
uses the same filter with scale $L_w$ and independent noise.

### Aerodynamic Disturbance Force

The wind disturbance force acting on the drone in the world frame is modelled as:

$$\mathbf{F}_w = \tfrac{1}{2}\,\rho_{air}\,C_D\,A_{ref}\;
  (\mathbf{V}_w - \mathbf{v}_d)\,|\mathbf{V}_w - \mathbf{v}_d|$$

where $\mathbf{V}_w = (0,\; W_y^{total},\; W_z^{turb})^\top$ is the wind velocity vector,
$\mathbf{v}_d = (v_x, v_y, v_z)^\top$ is the drone velocity, and the element-wise
$\mathbf{a}|\mathbf{a}|$ notation denotes $(a_x|a_x|,\; a_y|a_y|,\; a_z|a_z|)^\top$.
Air density $\rho_{air} = 1.225$ kg/m³, drag coefficient $C_D = 0.6$,
effective frontal area $A_{ref} = 0.04$ m².

### Linearised 3D Quadrotor Dynamics

The drone is modelled as a 6-DOF rigid body linearised about hover. The state vector is:

$$\mathbf{x} = \begin{pmatrix} p_x & p_y & p_z & v_x & v_y & v_z &
  \phi & \theta & \dot{\phi} & \dot{\theta} \end{pmatrix}^\top \in \mathbb{R}^{10}$$

The control input is $\mathbf{u} = (T,\; \tau_\phi,\; \tau_\theta)^\top$. The continuous-time
linearised plant about hover is $\dot{\mathbf{x}} = A\mathbf{x} + B\mathbf{u} + B_w\mathbf{F}_w$,
with the disturbance input matrix:

$$B_w = \mathrm{blkdiag}\!\left(\mathbf{0}_3,\; \tfrac{1}{m}\mathbf{I}_3,\; \mathbf{0}_4\right)$$

so that wind forces enter directly as linear accelerations on $(v_x, v_y, v_z)$.

### LQR Optimal Gain

The LQR gain $K \in \mathbb{R}^{3 \times 10}$ is found by solving the continuous algebraic Riccati
equation (CARE):

$$A^\top P + PA - PBR^{-1}B^\top P + Q = 0, \qquad K = R^{-1}B^\top P$$

The nominal LQR control law (without feedforward) is:

$$\mathbf{u}_{lqr}(t) = -K\,\mathbf{e}(t), \qquad \mathbf{e}(t) = \mathbf{x}(t) - \mathbf{x}_{ref}(t)$$

### RLS Wind Disturbance Estimator

The onboard RLS estimator models the wind disturbance as a slowly-varying acceleration bias
$\hat{\mathbf{w}}_k \in \mathbb{R}^3$ (one scalar per axis). At each timestep $k$ the estimator
compares the measured acceleration $\mathbf{a}_{meas}$ (from an IMU) with the model-predicted
acceleration $\mathbf{a}_{model}$ and updates the estimate:

$$\hat{\mathbf{w}}_{k+1} = \hat{\mathbf{w}}_k + \Gamma\,\bigl(\mathbf{a}_{meas,k}
  - \mathbf{a}_{model,k} - \hat{\mathbf{w}}_k\bigr)$$

where $\Gamma \in (0,1)$ is the forgetting gain (lower $\Gamma$ = slower but smoother adaptation).
The feedforward cancellation command is:

$$\mathbf{u}_{ff} = -m\,\hat{\mathbf{w}}_k$$

The combined LQR + RLS control law is therefore:

$$\mathbf{u}(t) = -K\,\mathbf{e}(t) + \mathbf{u}_{ff}(t)$$

### Attitude Limit Protection

Before the thrust command is applied, roll and pitch are checked against the structural limit
$\theta_{lim} = 30°$. If the estimated roll $\phi$ or pitch $\theta$ would exceed this bound, the
lateral thrust components are clamped:

$$\text{if } \|(\phi, \theta)\|_\infty > \theta_{lim}: \quad
  \tau_{\phi} \leftarrow \tau_\phi \cdot \frac{\theta_{lim}}{|\phi|}, \quad
  \tau_{\theta} \leftarrow \tau_\theta \cdot \frac{\theta_{lim}}{|\theta|}$$

Mission failure is triggered at the first instant where any angle exceeds $\theta_{fail} = 40°$ or
lateral position error exceeds $e_{y,fail} = 5$ m.

### Straight-Line Reference Trajectory

The reference trajectory advances along the $x$-axis at constant speed $v_{ref} = 1.0$ m/s:

$$\mathbf{p}_{ref}(t) = \begin{pmatrix} v_{ref}\,t \\ 0 \\ z_{ref} \end{pmatrix}, \qquad
  \mathbf{v}_{ref}(t) = \begin{pmatrix} v_{ref} \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{pmatrix}$$

with $z_{ref} = 10$ m and the mission complete when $p_x \geq 100$ m.

### Performance Metrics

| Metric | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| RMS lateral deviation | $\sqrt{\tfrac{1}{N}\sum_k y_k^2}$ |
| Peak lateral deviation | $\max_k |y_k|$ |
| Peak attitude angle | $\max_k \max(|\phi_k|, |\theta_k|)$ |
| RLS convergence time | First $t$ such that $|\hat{w}_y - \bar{W}_{y,eff}| < 0.5$ m/s² |
| Mission success | 1 if $p_x \geq 100$ m without failure, else 0 |

## Implementation

```python
import numpy as np
import matplotlib.pyplot as plt
from mpl_toolkits.mplot3d import Axes3D
from matplotlib.animation import FuncAnimation
from scipy.linalg import solve_continuous_are

# ── Physical constants ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
G           = 9.81      # m/s²
MASS        = 1.0       # kg
RHO_AIR     = 1.225     # kg/m³
C_D         = 0.6       # aerodynamic drag coefficient
A_REF       = 0.04      # m²  effective frontal area
I_XX        = 0.01      # kg·m²  roll inertia
I_YY        = 0.01      # kg·m²  pitch inertia

# ── Wind / Dryden parameters ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
MEAN_WIND_Y = 12.0      # m/s  mean lateral wind speed
GUST_SIGMA  = 4.0       # m/s  Dryden gust standard deviation
L_W         = 200.0     # m    turbulence scale length
V_AIRSPEED  = 12.0      # m/s  nominal airspeed for Dryden filter scaling

# ── Mission parameters ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Z_REF       = 10.0      # m    target altitude
V_REF       = 1.0       # m/s  forward reference speed
X_GOAL      = 100.0     # m    mission end position
THETA_LIM   = np.deg2rad(30.0)  # rad  attitude soft clamp limit
THETA_FAIL  = np.deg2rad(40.0)  # rad  attitude mission-fail threshold
E_Y_FAIL    = 5.0       # m    lateral deviation mission-fail threshold

# ── RLS parameters ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
GAMMA       = 0.05      # forgetting gain (0 < Gamma < 1)

# ── LQR weights ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
# State: [px, py, pz, vx, vy, vz, phi, theta, dphi, dtheta]
Q_DIAG = np.array([1.,  20., 20.,   # position  (lateral/vertical weighted high)
                   0.5,  4.,  4.,   # velocity
                   8.,   8.,         # attitude
                   1.,   1.])        # angular rate
R_DIAG = np.array([0.1, 1.0, 1.0])  # [thrust dev, tau_phi, tau_theta]

# ── Simulation ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
DT          = 0.02      # s  integration timestep
T_MAX       = 120.0     # s  mission timeout


# ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
# Dryden turbulence model (discrete state-space realisation)
# ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

class DrydenGust:
    """
    Second-order Dryden lateral/vertical gust filter.
    State: [x1, x2]; Output: W_turb (m/s).
    """
    def __init__(self, sigma, L, V, dt, seed=None):
        self.dt  = dt
        tau      = L / V
        # Continuous-time companion form
        Ac = np.array([[-2.0 / tau, -1.0 / tau**2],
                       [1.0,          0.0]])
        Bc = np.array([[1.0], [0.0]])
        Cc = sigma * np.sqrt(2.0 * V / (np.pi * L)) * np.array(
            [[np.sqrt(3.0) / tau, 1.0 / tau**2]])
        # Zero-order-hold discretisation
        from scipy.linalg import expm
        n = 2
        M = np.zeros((n + 1, n + 1))
        M[:n, :n] = Ac * dt
        M[:n,  n] = Bc[:, 0] * dt
        eM = expm(M)
        self.Ad = eM[:n, :n]
        self.Bd = eM[:n, n:n+1]
        self.Cd = Cc
        self.x  = np.zeros((n, 1))
        self.rng = np.random.default_rng(seed)

    def step(self):
        """Advance one timestep; return scalar gust velocity (m/s)."""
        noise   = self.rng.standard_normal((1, 1)) / np.sqrt(self.dt)
        self.x  = self.Ad @ self.x + self.Bd * noise
        return float(self.Cd @ self.x)


# ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
# Linear system construction and LQR
# ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

def build_linear_system():
    """
    Construct continuous-time matrices A (10x10), B (10x3), Bw (10x3).
    State: [px, py, pz, vx, vy, vz, phi, theta, dphi, dtheta]
    Input: [T (thrust deviation), tau_phi, tau_theta]
    Wind: [Fw_x, Fw_y, Fw_z]
    """
    n, m = 10, 3
    A  = np.zeros((n, n))
    B  = np.zeros((n, m))
    Bw = np.zeros((n, 3))

    # Position kinematics
    A[0, 3] = 1.0
    A[1, 4] = 1.0
    A[2, 5] = 1.0

    # Velocity dynamics (linearised hover)
    A[3, 7] =  G           # dvx = g * theta
    A[4, 6] = -G           # dvy = -g * phi
    B[5, 0] =  1.0 / MASS  # dvz = T/m

    # Attitude kinematics
    A[6, 8] = 1.0
    A[7, 9] = 1.0

    # Attitude dynamics
    B[8, 1] = 1.0 / I_XX
    B[9, 2] = 1.0 / I_YY

    # Wind force enters as linear acceleration on vx, vy, vz
    Bw[3, 0] = 1.0 / MASS
    Bw[4, 1] = 1.0 / MASS
    Bw[5, 2] = 1.0 / MASS

    return A, B, Bw


def compute_lqr_gain(A, B):
    """Solve CARE and return LQR gain K (3x10)."""
    Q = np.diag(Q_DIAG)
    R = np.diag(R_DIAG)
    P = solve_continuous_are(A, B, Q, R)
    return np.linalg.solve(R, B.T @ P)


def wind_force(drone_vel, wind_vel):
    """Quadratic aerodynamic disturbance force in world frame (N)."""
    dv = wind_vel - drone_vel
    return 0.5 * RHO_AIR * C_D * A_REF * dv * np.abs(dv)


def reference_state(t):
    """Return 10-element reference state for straight-line x-axis flight."""
    px = min(V_REF * t, X_GOAL)
    xr = np.zeros(10)
    xr[0] = px
    xr[2] = Z_REF
    xr[3] = V_REF if px < X_GOAL else 0.0
    return xr


# ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
# Simulation engine
# ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

def run_simulation(mode='lqr_rls', seed=42):
    """
    Simulate 100 m straight-line wind endurance flight.
    mode: 'pid' | 'lqr' | 'lqr_rls'
    Returns log dict and outcome dict.
    """
    A, B, Bw = build_linear_system()
    K = compute_lqr_gain(A, B)

    # Dryden gust generators (independent lateral + vertical)
    gust_y = DrydenGust(sigma=GUST_SIGMA, L=L_W, V=V_AIRSPEED, dt=DT, seed=seed)
    gust_z = DrydenGust(sigma=GUST_SIGMA * 0.5, L=L_W * 0.5, V=V_AIRSPEED,
                        dt=DT, seed=seed + 1)

    # Initial state: hover at start, zero velocity
    x = np.zeros(10)
    x[2] = Z_REF

    # RLS estimator state
    w_hat = np.zeros(3)   # estimated wind acceleration (m/s²)

    # PID state (position controller using simple gains)
    pid_int = np.zeros(3)
    KP_pid  = np.array([2.0, 4.0, 5.0])
    KI_pid  = np.array([0.0, 1.0, 1.0])
    KD_pid  = np.array([1.5, 3.0, 3.0])
    prev_e  = np.zeros(3)

    t  = 0.0
    failed     = False
    fail_reason = ''
    log = dict(t=[], pos=[], vel=[], att=[], w_hat=[], w_true=[],
               drift_y=[], u=[])

    while t < T_MAX:
        x_ref = reference_state(t)

        # ── Wind realisation ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
        Wy_turb = gust_y.step()
        Wz_turb = gust_z.step()
        Vw = np.array([0.0, MEAN_WIND_Y + Wy_turb, Wz_turb])
        Fw = wind_force(x[3:6], Vw)

        # ── Control law ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
        if mode == 'pid':
            e_pos = x[0:3] - x_ref[0:3]
            d_pos = (e_pos - prev_e) / DT
            pid_int += e_pos * DT
            pid_int = np.clip(pid_int, -5.0, 5.0)
            acc_cmd = -(KP_pid * e_pos + KI_pid * pid_int + KD_pid * d_pos)
            prev_e = e_pos.copy()
            # Map acceleration commands to pseudo thrust/torques
            u = np.array([
                MASS * (acc_cmd[2] + G),        # thrust
                MASS * (-acc_cmd[1]) / G * 5.0, # tau_phi
                MASS * ( acc_cmd[0]) / G * 5.0, # tau_theta
            ])

        else:  # LQR or LQR+RLS
            e = x - x_ref
            u_lqr = -K @ e

            if mode == 'lqr_rls':
                # RLS update: measured accel vs model accel
                a_model = np.array([G * x[7], -G * x[6],
                                    u_lqr[0] / MASS - G])
                a_meas  = a_model + Fw / MASS  # treat Fw/m as residual
                w_hat  += GAMMA * (a_meas - a_model - w_hat)
                u_ff    = np.zeros(3)
                u_ff[0] = -MASS * w_hat[2]  # vertical feedforward via thrust
                # Lateral feedforward via attitude torques
                u_ff[1] = -MASS * w_hat[1] / G * 5.0
                u_ff[2] =  MASS * w_hat[0] / G * 5.0
                u = u_lqr + u_ff
            else:
                u = u_lqr

        # ── Attitude soft clamp ───────────────────────────────────────────────
        if abs(x[6]) > THETA_LIM:
            u[1] *= THETA_LIM / (abs(x[6]) + 1e-9)
        if abs(x[7]) > THETA_LIM:
            u[2] *= THETA_LIM / (abs(x[7]) + 1e-9)

        # Thrust saturation
        u[0] = np.clip(u[0], 0.0, MASS * G * 2.5)

        # ── Dynamics integration (Euler) ──────────────────────────────────────
        acc = np.array([G * x[7], -G * x[6], u[0] / MASS - G]) + Fw / MASS
        alpha_phi   = u[1] / I_XX
        alpha_theta = u[2] / I_YY

        x[0:3] += x[3:6] * DT
        x[3:6] += acc * DT
        x[6]   += x[8] * DT
        x[7]   += x[9] * DT
        x[8]   += alpha_phi   * DT
        x[9]   += alpha_theta * DT

        # ── Logging ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
        log['t'].append(t)
        log['pos'].append(x[0:3].copy())
        log['vel'].append(x[3:6].copy())
        log['att'].append(x[6:8].copy())
        log['w_hat'].append(w_hat.copy())
        log['w_true'].append(Vw.copy())
        log['drift_y'].append(float(x[1]))
        log['u'].append(u.copy())

        # ── Failure checks ────────────────────────────────────────────────────
        if abs(x[1]) > E_Y_FAIL:
            failed = True
            fail_reason = f'lateral deviation {x[1]:.2f} m > {E_Y_FAIL} m'
            break
        if max(abs(x[6]), abs(x[7])) > THETA_FAIL:
            failed = True
            fail_reason = (f'attitude {np.rad2deg(max(abs(x[6]),abs(x[7]))):.1f}° '
                           f'> {np.rad2deg(THETA_FAIL):.0f}°')
            break

        # ── Mission complete ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
        if x[0] >= X_GOAL:
            break

        t += DT

    # Convert logs
    for k in ('pos', 'vel', 'att', 'w_hat', 'w_true', 'u'):
        log[k] = np.array(log[k]) if log[k] else np.zeros((0, 3))
    for k in ('t', 'drift_y'):
        log[k] = np.array(log[k])

    pos_arr = log['pos']
    att_arr = log['att']
    outcome = {
        'mode':           mode,
        'success':        not failed and (x[0] >= X_GOAL),
        'failed':         failed,
        'fail_reason':    fail_reason,
        'rms_lateral':    float(np.sqrt(np.mean(log['drift_y']**2)))
                          if len(log['drift_y']) else np.nan,
        'peak_lateral':   float(np.max(np.abs(log['drift_y'])))
                          if len(log['drift_y']) else np.nan,
        'peak_attitude_deg': float(np.rad2deg(np.max(np.abs(att_arr))))
                             if len(att_arr) else np.nan,
        'mission_time_s': float(log['t'][-1]) if len(log['t']) else np.nan,
    }
    return log, outcome

def plot_3d_wind_trajectory(log):
    """
    Dedicated 3D trajectory figure showing the drone path coloured by lateral
    deviation magnitude; wind arrows overlaid at sample points.
    """
    pos = log['pos']
    if not len(pos):
        return
    drift = np.abs(log['drift_y'])

    fig = plt.figure(figsize=(10, 8))
    ax  = fig.add_subplot(111, projection='3d')

    # Colour the path by lateral deviation
    from matplotlib.collections import LineCollection
    from mpl_toolkits.mplot3d.art3d import Line3DCollection
    points  = pos.reshape(-1, 1, 3)
    segs    = np.concatenate([points[:-1], points[1:]], axis=1)
    lc      = Line3DCollection(segs, cmap='plasma', linewidth=2)
    lc.set_array(drift[:-1])
    ax.add_collection(lc)
    fig.colorbar(lc, ax=ax, label='|y deviation| (m)', shrink=0.5)

    ax.quiver(pos[idx, 0], pos[idx, 1], pos[idx, 2],

              color='royalblue', alpha=0.5, length=1.0, label='Wind vector')

    ax.set_xlabel('x (m)')
    ax.set_ylabel('y (m)')
    ax.set_zlabel('z (m)')

def animate_flight(log, mode_name):
    """
    Animate the drone straight-line flight (top-down XY view).
    Trail is colour-coded by lateral deviation; wind bar shown on the side.
    """
    pos    = log['pos']
    drift  = log['drift_y']
    w_true = log['w_true']
    t_arr  = log['t']
    n      = len(pos)
    if n == 0:
        return
    step = max(1, n // 250)

    fig, (ax_xy, ax_info) = plt.subplots(1, 2, figsize=(14, 6),
                                          gridspec_kw={'width_ratios': [3, 1]})
    fig.suptitle(f'S084 Wind Endurance — {mode_name}', fontsize=11)

    ax_xy.set_xlim(-5, X_GOAL + 5)
    ax_xy.set_ylim(-E_Y_FAIL - 1, E_Y_FAIL + 1)
    ax_xy.axhline( E_Y_FAIL, color='black', linestyle='--', linewidth=1.0)
    ax_xy.axhline(-E_Y_FAIL, color='black', linestyle='--', linewidth=1.0)
    ax_xy.axhline(0, color='green', linestyle=':', linewidth=1.0)
    ax_xy.set_xlabel('x (m)')
    ax_xy.set_ylabel('y deviation (m)')
    ax_xy.set_title('Top-down XY view')

    trail, = ax_xy.plot([], [], 'crimson', linewidth=1.5)
    dot,   = ax_xy.plot([], [], 'ro', markersize=9)
    txt    = ax_xy.text(5, E_Y_FAIL - 0.4, '', fontsize=9)

    # Wind bar panel
    ax_info.set_xlim(0, 1)
    ax_info.set_ylim(0, MEAN_WIND_Y * 2)
    ax_info.set_xticks([])
    ax_info.set_ylabel('Wind speed (m/s)')
    ax_info.set_title('Lateral wind $W_y$')
    wind_bar = ax_info.bar([0.5], [0], width=0.6,
                            color='royalblue', alpha=0.7)[0]
    ax_info.axhline(MEAN_WIND_Y, color='navy', linestyle='--', linewidth=1.0)
    ax_info.text(0.5, MEAN_WIND_Y + 0.3, f'Mean {MEAN_WIND_Y} m/s',
                 ha='center', fontsize=8)

    def init():
        trail.set_data([], [])
        dot.set_data([], [])
        wind_bar.set_height(0)
        return trail, dot, txt, wind_bar

    def update(frame):
        idx = min(frame * step, n - 1)
        trail.set_data(pos[:idx, 0], pos[:idx, 1])
        dot.set_data([pos[idx, 0]], [pos[idx, 1]])
        wy  = float(w_true[idx, 1])
        txt.set_text(f't={t_arr[idx]:.1f}s  y={drift[idx]:.2f}m  Wy={wy:.1f}m/s')
        wind_bar.set_height(max(0, wy))
        return trail, dot, txt, wind_bar

    anim = FuncAnimation(fig, update, frames=n // step,
                         init_func=init, blit=True, interval=40)
    import os
    os.makedirs('outputs/05_special_entertainment/s084_wind_endurance', exist_ok=True)
    fname = (f'outputs/05_special_entertainment/s084_wind_endurance/'
             f'flight_{mode_name.lower().replace(" + ", "_").replace(" ", "_")}.gif')
    anim.save(fname, writer='pillow', fps=25)
    plt.close()
    print(f'Animation saved: {fname}')
```

## Extensions

1. **Adaptive forgetting gain $\Gamma$**: replace the fixed forgetting gain with a variable-gain
   RLS (VG-RLS) that increases $\Gamma$ when the innovation is large and decreases it once the
   estimate has converged; compare convergence speed and steady-state noise versus fixed $\Gamma$.
2. **EKF wind estimator**: replace the scalar RLS with a full Extended Kalman Filter that jointly
   estimates drone state and 3D wind vector; evaluate estimation accuracy under rapid gust
   direction changes (e.g. wind shear layer crossing).
3. **Robust $H_\infty$ controller**: design an $H_\infty$ controller that explicitly minimises
   worst-case lateral deviation over all Dryden realisations with $\sigma_w \leq 6$ m/s; compare
   with the LQR+RLS strategy in terms of conservatism and steady-state performance.
4. **Monte Carlo mission success rate**: run 500 random Dryden seeds for each controller; compute
   mission success rate, mean RMS deviation, and 95th-percentile peak attitude; produce a
   violin-plot comparison for certification-grade validation.
5. **Variable wind direction**: replace the fixed lateral wind with a slowly rotating wind direction
   (full 360° over the mission); assess whether the RLS estimator can track the changing mean wind
   vector and maintain mission success.
